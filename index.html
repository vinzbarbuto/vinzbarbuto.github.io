About

Let me introduce myself.

I'm Vincenzo Barbuto, a Ph.D. Candidate in Information and Communication Technologies at the Department of Computer, Modeling, Electronic, and System Engineering (DIMES), University of Calabria, Italy. I am member of Smart, Pervasive and Mobile systems Engineering (SPEME) Laboratory since November 2023. My research focuses on developing and implementing AI techniques that can operate directly on data sources, such as sensors and IoT devices, to enable real-time and efficient decision-making at the network edge.
